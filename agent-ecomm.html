Everything you need to know about Eeri

Will people want to talk to AI?

Yes! AI technology, especially voice AI, has advanced significantly. It's now human-like, fast, and enjoyable to interact with. Customers appreciate real-time solutions, and Eeri offers that.

How come I haven't heard about this before?

Almost no one is calling customers because it didn't used to be possible. AI was once robotic and slow. But now, voice AI is human-like and fast enough to be genuinely useful. Cart recovery with phone calls has always been effective but was too resource-intensive. With Eeri, it's on autopilot.

Does this make sense for my business?

It depends on your product. Eeri works best for businesses with higher-ticket items, recurring purchases (like supplements), or products that require some technical knowledge. If your business fits one of these, you're in a good spot. If you hit multiple, you're golden.

Won't this cheapen my brand?

Not if used correctly. Don't overdo it. We recommend only calling once per customer. If they don't answer or reply, leave them be. We only call during business hours (9 AM - 5 PM) in their timezone to ensure professionalism.

How many people actually leave their phone numbers?

Around 75% of customers provide their phone number, often because browsers auto-fill this information. If you're hiding the phone number field, you're leaving both money and valuable customer feedback on the table.

Don't people hate being bothered over the phone?

Not in this case. They're okay with it if you're solving their problem. These aren't cold calls – they're HOT calls. Customers have already shown interest by filling out forms on your website, so you're simply following up on their needs.
